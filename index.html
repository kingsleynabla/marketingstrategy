Change Your Style

by King Eazie


“Invisible Viral Audio Marketing” Campaign

Concept

Objective

Promote the song organically by embedding it into highly entertaining short-form content instead of directly advertising the track.

Core Idea

Use funny reaction videos with the song playing subtly in the background so audiences repeatedly hear the music while consuming entertaining content.

The goal is for listeners to subconsciously memorize the melody and associate it with humor, excitement, and replayable moments.

Campaign Strategy

Phase 1 — Invisible Exposure

Introduce the song naturally without audiences feeling they are being marketed to.

Execution

  • Collect 100+ short funny clips
  • Clip length: 15–35 seconds
  • Minimal or no talking
  • Humor must be visual
  • Add reactions/commentary visuals
  • Play song subtly in background

Content Types

  • Funny fails
  • Animal videos
  • Unexpected moments
  • Meme edits
  • Dance clips
  • Awkward public moments
  • Internet humor

Audio Strategy

Use One Catchy Section Repeatedly

Instead of using the full song, repeatedly use one memorable hook, rhythm section, or recognizable vocal moment.

Focus

  • One memorable hook
  • One rhythm section
  • One recognizable vocal moment

Purpose

  • Creates familiarity
  • Builds subconscious memorization
  • Increases audience recognition
  • Creates emotional attachment

Algorithm Strategy

Platform Distribution

Main Platforms

  • YouTube Shorts
  • TikTok
  • Instagram Reels
  • Facebook Reels

Upload Frequency

  • 3–8 posts daily
  • Same audio section consistently
  • Different visuals every upload

Platforms begin associating the sound with high retention, replay value, engagement, and repeated audio usage.

Viral Psychology

Why This Works

Emotional Association

  • Humor
  • Excitement
  • Entertainment
  • Dopamine/reward moments

Repetition Effect

  • Subconscious memorization
  • Recognition familiarity
  • Curiosity about the song
  • Emotional connection

Meme Conversion Phase

Turning the Song Into Internet Culture

Next-Step Content

  • Meme edits
  • Dance trends
  • POV videos
  • Sped-up versions
  • Slowed + reverb versions
  • Remix edits
  • Challenge videos

Goal

Transform the song from “background music” into a recognizable internet sound.

Final Vision

Do not market the song directly. Market entertainment, replay value, humor, and emotional connection. The audience discovers and remembers the song naturally through repeated enjoyable experiences.

Iyeye

by King Eazie


Traditional Cultural Awareness Campaign

Campaign Concept

Objective

Promote the song “Iyeye” by reconnecting with cultural roots, traditional leadership, and community identity through authentic storytelling and social media documentation.

Core Idea

Create short-form videos of visits to chiefs, elders, traditional homes, and cultural events while the song plays naturally in the background.

The strategy positions the song as a symbol of culture, pride, identity, and tradition while building awareness for the upcoming December Traditional Concert.

Content Strategy

Video Content

  • Greeting chiefs and elders
  • Traditional ceremonies
  • Community visits
  • Cultural storytelling moments
  • Traditional dance clips
  • Behind-the-scenes village moments

Platforms

  • TikTok
  • Instagram Reels
  • Facebook Reels
  • YouTube Shorts

Traditional Identity Strategy

Emotional Positioning

Audience Connection

  • Cultural pride
  • Respect for tradition
  • Authenticity
  • Community identity
  • Heritage appreciation

Visual Identity

  • Traditional outfits
  • Royal greetings
  • Village environments
  • Cultural symbols
  • Traditional music atmosphere

December Concert Awareness

Event Marketing Integration

Every short-form video also acts as awareness content for the upcoming December Traditional Concert.

Awareness Goals

  • Build anticipation
  • Create cultural movement
  • Grow local support
  • Increase online engagement
  • Strengthen artist identity

Video Style

  • Short cinematic clips
  • Emotional storytelling
  • Authentic interactions
  • Traditional soundscapes
  • Community-focused visuals

Expected Outcome

“Iyeye” becomes more than a song — it becomes a cultural experience tied to identity, tradition, and community celebration.

Final Vision

Build a long-term cultural brand around music, tradition, and authentic community engagement while using social media to connect heritage with modern digital audiences.
